Good questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost? +
Pre-registering for early access is 100% free. At launch there's no cash outlay for campaigns. You only sponsor the complimentary meal, essentially your raw ingredient cost, in exchange for authentic content and exposure.
What kind of creators will visit my restaurant? +
Real Malaysian foodie creators, food vloggers, and micro-influencers (typically 1K to 10K followers) with trusted local audiences in your city. Every applicant is approved by our team before they can apply to you.
Can I control what they post? +
Yes. You set the brief when you create a campaign (e.g. "highlight our signature nasi lemak" or "show the casual dining vibe"), and you only host the creators you approve. We do always encourage honest reviews, because that's what builds real customer trust.
What if I've never worked with influencers before? +
No worries at all. Share Eat! is built for busy restaurant owners with zero influencer-marketing experience. The platform handles vetting, briefs, and post tracking step by step, so you can focus on your food.
How is this different from hiring a marketing agency? +
Agencies charge RM2,000 to RM5,000+ a month on long contracts just to manage one channel or run ads. Share Eat! automates creator collaboration, giving you a steady stream of fresh content and wide local reach, funded purely by your kitchen.
