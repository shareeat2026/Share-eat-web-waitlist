Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
Pre-registering for early access is 100% free. Once we launch, there is no cash layout for campaigns—you only sponsor the complimentary meal (essentially your raw ingredients cost) to get authentic content and exposure.
What kind of creators will visit my restaurant?
Real Malaysian foodie creators, food vloggers, and micro-influencers (typically 1K to 10K followers) who have trusted local audiences in your specific city. Every applicant is fully approved by our team before they can apply.
Can I control what they post?
Yes. When setting up your campaign, you define your brief (e.g., "highlight our signature nasi lemak" or "show the casual dining vibe"). Plus, you only host the creators you review and choose to approve. But we always advocate for honest reviews to build genuine customer trust!
What if I've never worked with influencers before?
No worries at all. Share Eat! is built specifically for busy restaurant owners with no influencer marketing experience. The system handles application vetting, content briefs, and post tracking for you step-by-step so you can focus completely on your food.
How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?
Traditional agencies charge RM2,000–RM5,000+ per month with long duration contracts just to manage a single social media channel or run ads. Share Eat! automates influencer collaboration, giving you a continuous stream of fresh creator content and wide local reach funded purely by your kitchen.
