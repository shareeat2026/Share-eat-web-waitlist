Good questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a big following? +
No, lah! We focus on micro-influencers and rising creators (typically 1K to 10K followers). If your content looks good and your audience is active, Malaysian brands want to host you.
Is it actually free? +
Yes, 100% free for foodie creators. No membership fees, no hidden platform cuts. You bring authentic creativity; the restaurant serves your meal on the house.
What restaurants are on the platform? +
We're partnering with dining spots and cafés across the Klang Valley, from artisanal burger joints and aesthetic matcha spaces to hotpot and fine dining.
How do I get approved for campaigns? +
Super simple. Once you join, browse the campaign feed and apply to ones you like. Restaurants review your handles, check your audience vibe, and approve you directly in the app.
How is this different from DMing restaurants myself? +
DMing is a headache. You get ghosted or stuck with an intern who has zero authority. On Share Eat!, every listed restaurant is actively searching and ready to host partners right now.
