Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a big following?
No, lah! We focus heavily on micro-influencers and rising creators (typically between 1K–10K followers). If your videos look good and your audience is active, Malaysian brands want to host you.
Is it actually free?
Yes, 100% free for foodie creators. No membership fees, no hidden platform cuts. You bring your raw, authentic creativity, the restaurant team serves your meal on the house.
What restaurants are on the platform?
We are actively partnering with dining spots and trendy cafes across Klang Valley, from artisanal burger joints and aesthetic matcha spaces to hotpots and fine dining.
How do I get approved for campaigns?
Super simple. Once you join, browse the campaign feed and apply to campaigns you like. Restaurants review your social handles, verify your audience vibe, and approve you directly in the platform.
How is this different from just DMing restaurants myself?
DMing is a massive headache. You get ghosted, or you deal with an intern with zero authority. On Share Eat!, every restaurant listed is actively searching and looking to host partners immediately.
